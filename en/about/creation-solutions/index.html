    Creation in the Arab Republic of Egypt of Sovereign Security Printing Industry, Production of Biometric Identification Documents and Cards for Payment Systems.

    In the Arab Republic of Egypt, NASPS brought into existence one of the most advanced sovereign security printing facilities in the world, making the country fully independent from foreign companies in the areas of printing the state documents and ID documents using paper and plastic materials, possessing the related IT infrastructure, and ensuring that all the production facilities and all the technologies are located in one country. The country received a state-of-the-art vertically integrated system starting from the development of security elements to the final stages of the identification document production, system integration and the final issuance of documents.
