    NASPS PROFILE

    NASPS is a leader and a trendsetter in the arena of ID-documents and IT-systems development and implementation

    NASPS provides to the government and state bodies the most recent and the most efficient solutions in document identification, systems for identification document personalization by ID-1 and ID-3 format, laser engraving, production of highly protected biometric passports and ID-cards, the creation of a digital government and integrated population registration systems.

    NASPS is the first organization in the world which holds scientific and industrial closed cycle consortium which has full potential and capability to produce polymer and paper-based electronic identification documents in a single engineering and manufacturing chain. It has developed and executed Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) elements which assure smooth, fast, and secure production and circulation of the electronic identification documents.

    NASPS proudly possesses vast amount of unique, advanced and innovative technologies in the arena of biometrics, high-security document production, information systems, lasers, polycarbonate, distinctive de-metalized holograms, personalized with I-Met technology application, contact and contactless chips, solutions in excise stamp accounting and control, and wide-ranging solutions for preventing counterfeiting and falsification of goods and documents. Furthermore, NASPS owns Personalization Bureau has the up-to-date equipment with an integrated personalization system of the high-security electronic documents “e-ID card” and “E-pass”, which contains all necessary software components for the production management system, blank accounting system, automated quality control system, and the commensurate graphics and electronic personalization of the document’s smart-module. The Personalization Bureau is fitted out with self-manufactured personalization systems and technology.

    Additionally, NASPS guarantees the provision of full maintenance and service on the basis of its own technology products and integration systems.
