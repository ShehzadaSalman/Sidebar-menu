A large number of companies and government organizations worldwide use the holographic elements it produces to protect security documents and products against counterfeiting and falsification. NASPS solutions enable government and corporate organizations to efficiently issue and distribute tax and control stamps to maximize revenues from sales of excisable and protected products.

    To protect luxury and high-end goods against counterfeiting, NASPS has developed a comprehensive solution, MEGAFLEX, which uses a high-security holographic label with RFID chip and Track & Trace Information System. MegaFlex advantages for labeling, branding and counterfeit protection of premium class goods include: ultimate brand protection (visual, hidden, electronic); high quality, modern, attractive appearance and unique holographic label design; flexible solutions for security elements selection; guarantee of physical and digital authentication; protection against reuse or duplication of labels; availability of comprehensive track and trace solutions for different goods; and products supply chain management. The technology platform includes a highly secured RFID label (RFID-chip, high-resolution hologram, 2D-code), portable reading and verification devices, and a track & trace system with a database of goods.

    • continuous numbering of protected products (option)
    • classic high resolution printing (option)
    • Portable reading and verification devices
    • RFID readers
    • 2D code reading software for regular smart phone
    • spectrum analyzer for hologram verification
    • track & trace system with database of goods.
