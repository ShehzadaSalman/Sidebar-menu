    The capabilities of NASPS enterprises allow manufacture of all kinds of plastic ID-1 format identification documents for use in public and commercial projects of any complexity. NASPS ID-cards are known for their reliability, durability, and availability of a wide range of security elements and electronic means of identification (electronic chip with contact, contactless and dual interface). High-precision laser engraving and perforation and the use of holograms make it impossible to forge the ID cards. It is possible to engrave any information, such as texts, photographs, and signatures.

    TECHNICAL CHARACTERISTICS OF ID CARD MANUFACTURED AT THE NASPS ENTEPRISES
    CARD DURABILITY

    ID-cards must endure drastic environmental changes (temperature extremes, high humidity, etc.). Up-to-date technologies and materials applied by the NASPS for the manufacture and personalization of documents ensure the production of a durable ID cards on a plastic base.

    CARD DESIGN

    The high resolution of ID card print, a contemporary design developed consistent with the position of all required fields, security features and information on a card create the harmonious appearance of a plastic ID. The specialists of the NASPS have all the necessary skills and experience for the development of the individual design of a card, taking into account all demands for such documents for any state or commercial project.

    CARD MATERIAL

    NASPS uses various materials for the manufacture of durable, flexible and damage protected ID-cards based on such materials as PVC, PVC/ABS blend, polycarbonate, and composite plastic.
