    The capabilities of NASPS allow to manufacture all kinds of plastic ID-1 format identification documents for public or private projects of any complexity. NASPS offers different types of electronic ID-cards, including national ID-card, residence permit, refugee card, immigrant card, voting card, driving license, vehicle registrationÂ certificate, vehicle inspection sticker, insurance card, pension card, social card, temporary employment permit, student ID, military ID, police ID, firearms license, and others.

    NASPS ID-cards are known for their reliability, durability and using wide range of security elements, they could be produced with secure ICs that have any communication interface (contact, contactless or dual). It is possible to engrave any type of information, such as text, photographs or signatures. High-resolution holograms and high-precision laser engraving and perforation make forging the ID cards impossible.

    TECHNICAL CHARACTERISTICS OF ID CARD MANUFACTURED BY NASPS
    CARD DURABILITYY

    ID-cards must endure drastic environmental changes (temperature extremes, high humidity, etc.). Up-to-date technologies and materials applied by NASPS for manufacturing and personalization of documents ensure the production of a durable ID cards on a plastic base.

    CARD DESIGN

    The high resolution of printing, a contemporary design developed consistent with the position of all required fields, security features and information on a card create the harmonious appearance of a plastic ID. The specialists of NASPS have all the necessary skills and experience to develop the design of a card, taking into account all requirements for such documents for any public or private project.

    CARD MATERIAL

    NASPS uses various materials to produce durable and secure ID-cards such as PVC, PVC/ABS blend, polycarbonate, and composite plastic.

    DOCUMENT DESCRIPTIONN

    Electronic Permanent Residence Permit issued by the Arab Republic of Egypt is a contactless smart card made of 100% polycarbonate. Its security elements include a high-resolution hologram, an embedded secure chip, blind embossing, secure print, laser engraving.
