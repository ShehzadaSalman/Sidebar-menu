    NASPS aims to become a world leader in the development of advanced technologies and manufacturing of passports and other identification documents. NASPS has developed the highest-level solutions for design of secure documents, which are based on many years of work by the world’s leading experts.

    The passport design is a combination of modern graphic art capabilities providing comprehensive protection of the document. The cover of the passport is made of fabric-based material and contains a contactless electronic chip. The use of multilayer polycarbonate material for the data page allows personalizing the documents with laser engraving and laser perforation. Laser engraving creates visual information inside the structure of the material, which practically eliminates the possibility of forgery. The wide range of security elements efficiently protects the document from counterfeiting and forgery.

    The brand-new electronic passport offered by the NASPS:

    • allows implementing documents fully complaint with requirements of ICAO- and other relevant standards;
    • improves security of the machine-readable travel documents;
    • fusion of the advanced design and manufacturing technologies, as well as materials and protection means;
    • compatible with unique methods of personalization;
    • assures reliability and efficiency of identification;
    • open for further improvements of the identification system.

    Security paper that NASPS uses for electronic passports:

    • has no intrinsic fluorescence;
    • contains double-tone watermark, visible and invisible fluorescent fibres;
    • chemically protected;
    • has high content of cotton fibres to improve durability of the document.

    The electronic seaman’s passport offered by NASPS is fully complaint with requirements of the Seafarers Identity Documents Convention, 2003 (No. 185 ) of the International Labour Organization.
