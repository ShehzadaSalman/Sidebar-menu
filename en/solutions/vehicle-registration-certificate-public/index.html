    NASPS offers an Electronic Vehicle Registration Card and system that complies with internationally accepted security standards. The document is made as a plastic card sized ID-1 (in compliance with ISO/IEC 7810:2003) on the multilayer polymeric substrate, and it is protected with a special surface layer enhancing its service capacity. The personalization of the Electronic Vehicle Registration Card can be performed on a centralized or decentralized basis.

    The application of unique technological modules ensures the issuance of documents with minimal expenditures and high protective properties. The card can contain an embedded electronic chip with all of the visual information used in the document. The document involves a combination of the most up to date security instruments for both graphic protection and security of data contained in the electronic chip. The document series and number are done using laser engraving. The card contains necessary security features such as anti-scanner background images, guilloche elements, other graphic images, micro text, and fluorescent inks.

    TECHNICAL FEATURES OF BLANK

    Plastic card is made of multilayer polymeric material.

    Card size – 54,0 х 85,6 (± 0,125) mm, thickness – 0,76 mm ± 0,08 mm, which correspond with the ID-1 type (ISO/IEC 7810:2003).

    Card material – polyvinylchloride. Production method – integration of material layers in a multilayer solid structure under the temperature and pressure.

    Physical and other features of ID-1 are in compliance with DSTU ISO/IEC 7810:2008.
