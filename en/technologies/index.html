    NASPS TECHNOLOGIES — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

    NASPS possess substantial intellectual property rights which allow them to offer comprehensive solutions to establish a complete cycle of production, circulation and functioning for national identification documents and forms of strict accountability.

    Specifically, NASPS enterprises have intellectual property rights in 240 areas, including:

    • Process for obtaining achromatic holographic images
    • Method of manufacturing a monolithic stack of perforated sheets of polycarbonate
    • Holographic element — «The lens hologram»
    • Multi-layered document protection
    • Active element of the solid-state quantum amplifier
    • Display device for forming images of quasi three-dimensional objects
    • Protective element for the securities and documents
    • Computer program — «IDCardMaster»
    • Set of computer programs which form the operating system for the chip card.
